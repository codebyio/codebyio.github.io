Partner Journey

1

Reaching Out

Planning a system upgrade or unsure which tools your team should use? If you are facing blockers, unclear workflows, or upcoming product launches, this is the time to consult. A quick message is all it takes to start uncovering the right technical direction.

2

Consultation

Our free consultation helps uncover where your technical strategy may need clarity. We ask key questions to understand your current system, infrastructure, and business priorities. This session lays the foundation for strategic planning and system improvement.

3

Technical Assessment

After learning your goals, we assess the technical landscape more deeply. This includes reviewing documentation, checking the system architecture, and identifying gaps in your build process or team roles. The result is a technical overview that’s clear, accurate, and useful for planning next steps.

4

Proposal and Direction

We then prepare a tailored Tech Consultancy proposal. It includes a detailed scope, timeline, deliverables, and suggested focus areas. Once approved and signed, we begin with aligned expectations and a structured direction.

5

Active Support

This is where the Tech Consultancy service moves into execution. We help align your system’s architecture, clarify technical roles, and support your team or vendor through structured guidance. When needed, we also assist in shaping the right tech team to match your project goals.

6

Handoff

At the end of our engagement, we deliver a full Tech Consultancy handover package. This can include updated documentation, diagrams, or roadmap instructions that your team or next vendor can follow easily. Everything is structured for a smooth transition.

7

Aftercare Support

Our Tech Consultancy aftercare support does not stop at handoff. We remain available for follow-up questions, light updates, or deeper involvement if your system evolves or new needs arise.
